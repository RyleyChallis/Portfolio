Cookies Policy

To provide a smooth and efficient user experience, this website uses minimal cookie technology. Unlike many modern platforms, I prioritise your privacy by avoiding intrusive tracking or third-party advertising cookies. This section explains the limited way in which cookies are used to ensure the technical performance and security of my hand-coded environment.

  • Essential Cookies: We only use "strictly necessary" cookies that allow the website to function properly.
  • No Tracking: This site does not use analytical or marketing cookies to track your behavior across other websites.
  • Performance: Cookies may be used to remember your preferences (such as language or region) to improve your visit.
  • Control: You can choose to disable cookies through your browser settings, though some features of the site may not function as intended.
  • Duration: Most cookies used are "session cookies," which are automatically deleted when you close your browser.
