How do you work?

Step 01: Strategy & Prep

We discuss your project goals and service type so I can get a sense of your business model. After, you hand over all assets I'll need for development, e.g. brand colours, media (logos, images, videos), all text, paragraphs, titles, etc.

Step 02: Build & Refine

Utilising clean, semantic HTML, CSS and JavaScript, I develop your site including all features defined in our contract.

Step 03: Launch & Support

Once you're happy with the design, I host your site using your custom domain via Cloudflare Pages. I also provide 14-30 day free maintenance for any of your project needs, i.e. image, text or colour changes.
