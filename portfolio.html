Tipsy Kisses — Luxury Mixology

View Live Site

For Tipsy Kisses, the goal was to capture the sophisticated yet vibrant energy of a high-end mobile mixology service. The brand specialises in curated cocktail experiences, so the website needed to feel like a digital extension of their physical presence—polished, professional, and visually enticing. I focused on a "clean luxury" aesthetic, utilising high-quality lifestyle photography paired with a minimalist layout to ensure the cocktails themselves remained the focal point of the user journey.

The site architecture was intentionally designed to streamline the booking process for corporate events and private parties. By implementing smooth scroll transitions and a bespoke navigation system, I ensured that potential clients could glide from viewing the drink gallery to checking package details without friction. The final result is a high-performance digital storefront that doesn’t just show off their skills, but actively converts local Sussex visitors into lifelong customers through a seamless, premium user experience.

Broadway Roofing

View Live Site

For this spec site, I took Broadway Roofing's poorly-designed website and transform it into a premium, efficient web app designed to convert browsers to clients.

The site's layout and design were meticulously crafted for brilliance. With an interactive, dynamic contact form with alternating input forms based on previous answers, I ensured all queries were tailored to a prospective client's exact needs. The services and former projects showcases were simple, frictionless and made to entice the user. This project's experience is unmatched in the industry, and is highly sought after.
