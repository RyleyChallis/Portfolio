Tipsy Kisses — Luxury Mixology

For Tipsy Kisses, the goal was to capture the sophisticated yet vibrant energy of a high-end mobile mixology service. The brand specializes in curated cocktail experiences, so the website needed to feel like a digital extension of their physical presence—polished, professional, and visually enticing. I focused on a "clean luxury" aesthetic, utilizing high-quality lifestyle photography paired with a minimalist layout to ensure the cocktails themselves remained the focal point of the user journey.

The site architecture was intentionally designed to streamline the booking process for corporate events and private parties. By implementing smooth scroll transitions and a bespoke navigation system, I ensured that potential clients could glide from viewing the drink gallery to checking package details without friction. The final result is a high-performance digital storefront that doesn’t just show off their skills, but actively converts local Sussex visitors into lifelong customers through a seamless, premium user experience.

Elate — Artisan Coffee Brand Identity

For Elate, the focus was on translating the tactile, sensory experience of artisan coffee into a digital format. The brand is built on the philosophy of "elevated simplicity," so the site layout mirrors that through generous white space and a sophisticated, earth-toned color palette. I prioritised high-resolution imagery of the brewing process to evoke the aroma and craft behind every cup, creating an immediate emotional connection with coffee enthusiasts before they even visit the shop.

The technical execution centered on creating an intuitive flow that guides users from discovery to community engagement. I developed a responsive menu system that highlights their rotating single-origin beans and seasonal offerings with high clarity across all devices. By integrating a subtle, boutique-style grid layout and refined typography, the site serves as a digital destination that feels as carefully crafted as the coffee itself, reinforcing Elate's position as a premium leader in the local Sussex coffee scene.
