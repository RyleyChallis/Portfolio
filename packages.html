Starter Pack

The Starter Pack is ideal for local freelancers, tradespeople, or independent brands needing a highly focused, professional two-page digital foundation. If you need a clean, high-impact launchpad without the bloat, this is for you.

  • Pages: This package allows two pages, including the home page. If you're unsure what the second one should be, I'd always recommend a product/services page.
  • Features: 100% responsiveness for all devices, basic SEO (meta tags), and a contact form linked to Google Sheets.
  • Revisions: The Starter Pack allows one round of minor revisions, such as typos and colour changes. This round lasts ip to 14 days from project start.
  • Pricing: Pricing starts at £450 but would increase if any extra features are requested. This increase would be introduced by my standard out-of-scope overage rate of £30/hr. If an out-of-scope feature is fully developed before the hour is complete, the £30 is scrapped and the overage fee is rounded to the nearest quarter hour.

Process Breakdown

1. Strategy & Preparation

This first stage occurs before I even open my code editor. The Strategy sub-step is when we discuss your business's vibes, aesthetic, main clients/projects and hopes for this project. The Preparation sub-step is where we discuss your site's actual design, including layout, colours, branding, copy (text e.g. paragraphs), and any other assets you'd like on your page.

2. Build & Refine

The Build & Refine stage is the stage in which I conduct the actual development. This usually takes between 2-4 weeks, but I may need more time depending on project size and features. Using clean, modern HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, I employ various engineering and designing skills I've learned to ensure your site captures the raw detail and quality of your brand.

3. Launch & Support

The Launch & Support stage is when I get your site live and offer 14-30 day free support. Using Cloudflare Pages, I upload all files to search engines and attach your custom domain name to make the site completely and uniquely yours. As stated, all packages come with free maintenance, with the Starter Pack allowing up to 14 days, and the Business and Bespoke Suites offering 30 days support.
