Starter Pack

The Starter Pack is perfect for local freelancers or tradespeople who need a clean, professional landing page or two to jumpstart their digital presence. If you need something simple, this one's for you.

  • Pages: You are entitled to two pages, including the home page. If you're unsure what the second one should be, I'd always recommend a product/services page.
  • Features: 100% responsiveness for all devices, basic SEO (meta tags), and a contact form linked to Google Sheets.
  • Revisions: The Starter Pack allows one round of minor revisions, such as typos and colour changes. This round can be applied within 14 days of project start.
  • Pricing: Pricing starts at £450, but may increase if you would like any extra features. This increase would be caused by my standard out-of-scope freelance rate of £30/hr.

Business Suite

The Business Suite is perfect for business which aren't so simple or complex, such as chain restaurants, ecommerce, and those that need automated assistants which filter out bad customers/clients and pass beneficial ones.

  • Pages: You are entitled to 3-5 strategic pages. These could be Home, About/Team, Services/Products, Contact + one of your own choice.
  • Features: A Google Maps Integration to display your business's location, Local SEO Kit (meta tags and JSON Schema), Interactive Client Contact/Onboarding Form linked to Google Sheets, and Analytics setup to track site impressions.
  • Revisions: The Business Suite allows one round of minor revisions, such as typos and colour changes. This round can be applied within 30 days of project start.
  • Pricing: Pricing starts at £700, but additional out-of-scope features (£30/hr) will push the fee.
